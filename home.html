Introducing Color Therapy Yoga

Welcome to the Colour Therapeutic Yoga website. Colour Therapy Yoga is a new revolutionary way to experience Yoga and meditation all the while, using colour alternating lamps, you summon the powerful colour chakras to your body in a most healing and beneficial way.

Classes usually start with the purple chakra and go through the whole spectrum of the chakras until finally ending in the red chakra (love) for the meditation finish. At home with an exclusive Colour Theraputic lamp, you can concentrate on the chakras you feel the most in need of.

Benefits

By combining the three arts together you benefit hugely, mind, body and spirit. The body, reduction in stress, the alleviation of health problems, improved mood and benefits to the suppleness of the spine that you get from the art of yoga.

The mind, the ease of depression and anxiety, improved concentration, reaction time and memory, the improvement of self-control and self-esteem, not to mention a reduction in high blood pressure that you get from meditation.

The spirit, the maximising of energy and the promotion of general health and well-being that you get from colour therapy.
