About Colour Therapeutic Yoga

Colour Therapeutic Yoga (CTY) is the unique, cutting-edge way to practice the art of yoga and meditation. By including the art of chronotherapy (colour therapy), you get a very powerful and spiritual discipline.

Combining the exercise and stretching benefits of yoga and the relaxation and centering of meditation with balancing and maximizing the flow of energy that colour therapy promotes, you are exercising in the most beneficial way, not only the body and mind, but the spirit as well.

Performing Colour Therapeutic Yoga

With the use of a colour therapeutic yoga lamp (CTY lamp), yoga and meditation is performed with the healing colours of the seven chakras of chronotherapy (CTY). You may set the lamp to whatever chakra colour you feel your body may be lacking, for example the yellow chakra or Third/Solar Plexus chakra which promotes calming emotions and frustrations, easing tension and helping to better utilize intuition, and keep this colour for a whole yoga and meditation session. What the classes consist of is running down the seven chakras, beginning with the Seventh/Crown (Indigo) chakra and moving down to the Second/Sacral (Orange) while performing a full range of yoga poses, the class then finishes with a ten minute meditation in the hue of the First/Root Chakra (Red), which gives energy to the physical body, controls fear and increase overall health.
